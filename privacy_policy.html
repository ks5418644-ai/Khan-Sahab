1. Introduction

Welcome to Rabiya Sufi AI ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how our mobile application handles, manages, and protects your data while ensuring compliance with global distribution standards (including Google Play Store, Aptoide Connect, and alternative app store distribution networks).

By downloading and utilizing the Rabiya Sufi AI application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Zero Data Harvesting Policy

We believe in absolute data privacy. Rabiya Sufi AI is designed to operate offline-first where possible.

  • Conversational History: All your chat transcripts with Rabiya are stored locally on your device in an encrypted SQLite database using the Jetpack Room database sandbox.
  • No Server Synced Accounts: The app does not require a user registration account to operate, meaning we do not store your profile, credentials, or private details on our servers.
  • No Data Selling: We never harvest, sell, rent, or share any of your private databases, custom prompts, or settings with third-party brokers.

3. Real-Time AI Processing & Secure Gateway

To process your advanced prompts, code generation, and voice requests, Rabiya Sufi AI interfaces with secure API endpoints (Google Gemini & OpenRouter gateways).

  • Your prompts are transmitted over secure, military-grade SSL pipelines (HTTPS over TLS 1.3).
  • These requests are strictly used to generate real-time AI answers and are not saved, cached, or compiled by intermediaries.

4. Device Permissions & Hardware Usage

To provide advanced vocal and media features, the app requests specific permissions. We only access these resources when you actively trigger them:

  • Record Audio (Microphone): Accessed strictly when you press the voice dictation or vocalizer mic buttons. This audio is processed instantly on-device for Text-to-Speech (TTS) and Speech-to-Text (STT). It is never recorded in the background or saved.
  • Camera & File Storage: Used only when you choose to attach an image, PDF, or text file to analyze inside the neural toolbox workspace.

5. Advertising & Analytics

To fund high-speed AI API pipelines and keep our services accessible, the app serves non-intrusive advertisements (banners, native banner cards, and rewarded video ads).

  • Standard advertising identifiers (such as the Google Advertising ID) may be accessed by compliant ad networks to deliver non-intrusive, secure ads.
  • These identifiers are handled strictly in accordance with Google Play and IAB privacy framework standards.

6. GDPR, UK, & CCPA Compliance

We respect your privacy rights across all regions:

  • EEA & UK (GDPR): European Union and United Kingdom users can toggle and reset ad personalization at any time, ensuring no digital footprints are generated.
  • California (CCPA): The app fully complies with CCPA rights. No personal details are sold or leased.
